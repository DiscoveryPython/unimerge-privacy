UniMerge Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information Collection

UniMerge does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to the developer.

2. Local File Access

UniMerge may ask you to select local files or folders so that you can import, arrange, merge, preview, and export documents on your device. Any files you choose are used only for processing on your device and are not uploaded to the developer.

3. Document Processing and Export

UniMerge is designed to process your selected content locally on your Mac. Imported files, page arrangements, preview data, and exported results are handled on-device.

If the app creates temporary working data during document processing, that data is used only to complete the requested operation on your device.

4. Network Access

UniMerge does not require developer-operated servers for its core document merge features. If any future feature involves opening a user-provided online resource or external link, your device would connect directly to the relevant third-party service, not through the developer’s server.

Third-party services may receive technical information such as your IP address as part of standard network communication. Their handling of information is governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Third-Party SDKs

UniMerge does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

6. Data Security

Your source files, processing results, export files, and related document data remain on your device unless you choose to save, move, or share them yourself. UniMerge does not upload your documents to the developer.
