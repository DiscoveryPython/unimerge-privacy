Welcome to UniMerge! If you encounter any issues while importing, arranging, merging, or exporting documents, please check the FAQ below first.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What can UniMerge do?

A: UniMerge helps you import multiple files, arrange their order or pages, preview the result, and export merged output in supported formats.

Q: Why can’t the app find or open my files?

A: Please make sure you granted file or folder access permissions, and try selecting the files again if needed.

Q: Why does the exported result look different from the source file?

A: Output appearance can vary depending on the source format, layout settings, page order, scaling, and export type you selected.

Q: How can I report a bug or request a feature?

A: Contact us by email or submit an issue via GitHub.
